Two products, one workspace

Built for sales teams and knowledge workers.

Notion Pilot deploys two independent products in your Notion. Use them from Telegram or directly in Notion — whichever fits your workflow.

notion-crm

A lightweight CRM for small sales teams (2–10 people). Manage your pipeline directly in Notion, or capture new contacts and deals on the go via Telegram commands.

  • People, Companies, Deals databases
  • Telegram: /lead /deal /company /enrich
  • Apollo.io + Brave Search enrichment
  • LLM field extraction from free-form messages

notion-inbox

A personal knowledge inbox. Manage your knowledge base in Notion, or send links, voice notes, and ideas from Telegram — they land structured and tagged automatically.

  • Knowledge, Ideas, Tools, Data & Tech databases
  • Auto-detects GitHub, YouTube, arXiv, podcasts…
  • Voice-to-text via faster-whisper (on-device)
  • LLM enrichment via OpenRouter (optional)
